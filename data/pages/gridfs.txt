=====About=====
GridFS is a specification for storing and retrieving files that exceed the BSON-document size limit of 16MB.
=====Where we use it=====
Freetable file servers rely on GridFS for file storage.
=====Why we use it=====
It's very simple to use and fits our needs perfectly!
